Our Services

What We Provide

  • Speech Impairment

    Difficulties in producing or understanding speech sounds, affecting communication clarity and fluency .

  • Hearing Impairement

    A partial or total inability to hear sounds, affecting communication and auditory perception.

  • Autism

    A neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by challenges in social communication, repetitive behaviors, and often, restricted interests or activities.

  • Speech And Language Therapy

    Helps improve communication skills and swallowing abilities through specialized interventions and exercises.

  • Speech And Language Delay

    It refers to slower-than-typical development in a child's ability to communicate effectively.

  • ADHD

    ADHD (Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder) is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by inattention, impulsivity, and hyperactivity, affecting daily functioning.

  • Learning Disability

    A neurological condition that affects the brain's ability to receive, process, store, and respond to information.

  • Mental Retardation

    "Mental retardation" was a previously used term that is now replaced with "intellectual disability," referring to limitations in cognitive and adaptive functioning.

  • Attention And Concentration Issue

    Refer to difficulties in focusing on tasks or sustaining attention over time, often associated with conditions like ADHD or cognitive impairments.

  • Multiple Disability

    Refer to the coexistence of two or more significant disabilities, such as physical, intellectual, sensory, or developmental disabilities, which impact various aspects of a person's functioning and require specialized support.

  • Assessment And Parent Counselling

    Involve evaluating a child's needs and providing guidance and support to parents.
